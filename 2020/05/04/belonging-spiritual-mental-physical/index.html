belonging (A-side: spiritual )

Svana Mjalli

belonging (spiritual)

Belonging has different meanings for all of us. What determines whether or not we feel a belonging towards a place or thing? And what does it mean to belong? This mixtape shows, through the use of interviews, the taxonomy of belonging, starting from confusion of belonging and what it is, ending in the realization that nothing belongs to you (spiritually).
