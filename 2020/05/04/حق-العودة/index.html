حق العودة

Yasmin, Obada

This mixtape is based on the Palestinian Nakba in 1948 and, how Palestinians migrated from their original and homeland countries. Al Azieh Camp which is located in Bethlehem is our focus. The purpose of shedding the light on a camp is for the reason that all the citizens that live in this camp obviously are migrants. The main goal of this mixtape is to spread awareness about hurtful memories of Nakba which leads so many people to leave in Diaspora and with no shelter or food. This is a reminder for the existing Palestinian leaders that Palestinians and especially refugees will not compromise over their lands which is as valuable as their children. We will spread this awareness through this mixtape so more people will be able to listen to it. As we also believe that often more people will listen to music and interview as a voice more than reading an article or a book that speaks up in the voice of Palestinians, especially refugees. This mixtape will be available for every Palestinian and non-Palestinian particularly, for the upcoming generation that will shape our world in a better and more peaceful way.
